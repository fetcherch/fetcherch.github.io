The Meaning Behind Dona.re

The name Dona.re comes from the Latin word donare, meaning “to give.” Historically, the word was used to describe acts of generosity, contribution, and offering value to others. Dona.re brings that idea to the modern internet — creating a simple way for people to support creators, founders, developers, and independent work online.



The Art of Giving

Giving online should feel personal, intentional, and meaningful. Dona.re is built around the idea that attention has value and that direct support creates stronger connections between people. Whether it’s a donation, a paid message, a tip, or a thoughtful inquiry, every interaction becomes more valuable when people choose to give intentionally.
